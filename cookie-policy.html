Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and to provide information to website owners about user behavior and preferences.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience on Aravali Store. Cookies help us:

  • Keep you logged in as you browse the site, so you don't have to re-enter your credentials on every page.
  • Remember your preferences, such as language, delivery address, and items in your cart.
  • Understand how you interact with our website, which pages you visit most, and how you navigate between sections.
  • Improve our website performance and user experience based on usage patterns.
  • Provide personalized product recommendations based on your browsing and purchase history.
  • Ensure the security of your session and detect unauthorized access.
  • Measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns and promotional offers.

Types of Cookies We Use

Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function properly. They enable core features like security, shopping cart functionality, and account authentication. Without these cookies, services you have requested cannot be provided.

Performance and Analytics Cookies

These cookies collect information about how visitors use our website, such as which pages are visited most often and any error messages encountered. All information collected is aggregated and anonymous. This data helps us improve the website's performance.

Functionality Cookies

These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make (such as your username, language, or region) and provide enhanced, personalized features. They may also be used to remember changes you have made to customizable parts of the site.

Marketing and Advertising Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver advertisements that are relevant to your interests. They also limit the number of times you see an ad and help measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns. They are placed by third-party advertising networks with our permission.

Third-Party Cookies

In addition to our own cookies, we may use cookies from trusted third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: To analyze website traffic and user behavior patterns.
  • Payment Gateways: To ensure secure payment processing and fraud prevention.
  • Social Media Plugins: To enable sharing features and social media integrations.
  • Customer Support: To provide live chat functionality and support services.

These third-party cookies are subject to the respective privacy policies of these providers.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have control over cookies. Here's how you can manage them:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Refer to your browser's help section for instructions. Note that blocking cookies may affect website functionality.
  • Opt-Out Links: You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on.
  • Cookie Banner: When you first visit our site, you can accept or decline non-essential cookies through the cookie consent banner.

Cookie Retention

Different cookies have different lifespans:

  • Session Cookies: These are temporary and are deleted from your device when you close your browser.
  • Persistent Cookies: These remain on your device for a set period (usually 30 days to 1 year) or until you delete them manually.

Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you choose to disable cookies, some features of the website may not function as intended:

  • You may need to log in repeatedly during a session.
  • Items in your cart may not be saved across sessions.
  • Personalized recommendations and content will not be available.
  • Some pages may load slower or display incorrectly.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legislation, or our business operations. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
