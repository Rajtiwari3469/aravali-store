Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about shopping with Aravali Store

How long does delivery take?

We offer same-day delivery for orders placed before 2:00 PM within Udaipur city. Orders placed after 2:00 PM will be delivered the next day. For areas outside Udaipur, delivery may take 2-5 business days depending on the location.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a wide range of payment methods including UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, RuPay), net banking, and Cash on Delivery (COD). All online transactions are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption.

What is your return and refund policy?

If you receive a damaged, expired, or wrong item, please raise a return request within 24 hours of delivery. Refunds for prepaid orders are processed within 3-5 business days to the original payment method. For COD orders, refunds are credited to your Aravali Store wallet. Perishable items like fresh fruits and vegetables can only be returned if they arrive damaged or spoiled.

How do I create or manage my account?

Click on the login icon in the navbar to sign up or log in. You can register using your email address or phone number. Once logged in, visit the Dashboard to manage your profile, view order history, update addresses, and change your password.

How do I place an order?

Simply browse our Shop or Categories pages, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can select your delivery address, choose a payment method, and apply any available coupon codes before placing the order. You'll receive a confirmation via email and SMS.

Are your products fresh and quality guaranteed?

Absolutely! We source our products directly from local farms and trusted suppliers. Our team carefully inspects every item before dispatch. Fruits and vegetables are sourced daily to ensure maximum freshness. If you're ever unsatisfied with the quality, contact us within 24 hours for a hassle-free replacement or refund.

Do you offer wholesale or bulk ordering?

Yes! We cater to bulk and wholesale orders for events, restaurants, and businesses. Please visit our Contact Us page or call us at +91 98765 43210 to discuss your requirements. Special pricing and dedicated support are available for bulk orders.

How do coupons and discounts work?

We regularly offer coupons and promotional discounts. Check the Offers page for current deals. You can apply a coupon code at checkout. Please note that only one coupon can be used per order and coupons cannot be combined with other ongoing promotions unless stated otherwise.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is placed, you can track its status from the My Orders section in your dashboard. You'll also receive real-time updates via SMS and email at each stage — order confirmed, out for delivery, and delivered.

Can I cancel my order?

Yes, you can cancel an order within 1 hour of placing it, provided it hasn't been dispatched yet. Go to My Orders in your dashboard and click the cancel button. For prepaid orders, the refund will be processed within 3-5 business days. If the order has already been dispatched, please refuse the delivery or raise a return request after receiving it.
