Refund & Return Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Overview

At Aravali Store, your satisfaction is our top priority. If you're not happy with your purchase, we're here to help. This policy outlines the conditions under which returns and refunds are accepted.

Return Window

  • Non-perishable items: You may request a return within 7 days of delivery.
  • Perishable items (fruits, vegetables, dairy, bakery): Must be reported within 24 hours of delivery if damaged, spoiled, or incorrect.
  • Frozen products: Must be reported within 24 hours if thawed, damaged, or incorrect.

To initiate a return, go to My Orders in your dashboard and select the item you wish to return, or contact our support team.

Eligible Items for Return

You may return items that are:

  • Damaged, broken, or defective upon delivery.
  • Expired or approaching immediate expiry at the time of delivery.
  • Not as described (wrong product, wrong quantity, or missing items).
  • Contaminated or not fit for consumption.

Non-Returnable Items

The following items are not eligible for return unless they arrive damaged or defective:

  • Personal care and hygiene products that have been opened.
  • Items with broken tamper seals.
  • Products that have been used, consumed, or altered after delivery.
  • Gift cards and digital vouchers.
  • Items purchased during clearance sales (unless defective).

How to Request a Return

  1. Log in to your account and go to My Orders.
  2. Select the order containing the item you want to return.
  3. Click "Return Item" and select the reason for return.
  4. Upload photos of the damaged or incorrect item (if applicable).
  5. Submit the request. Our team will review and respond within 24-48 hours.

Refund Process

Once your return is approved:

  • Prepaid orders: Refund is processed to the original payment method within 3-5 business days.
  • Cash on Delivery: Refund is credited to your Aravali Store wallet immediately upon approval, or to your bank account within 5-7 business days if requested.
  • Wallet balance: Can be used for future purchases or transferred to your bank account on request.

Replacement

If you prefer a replacement over a refund, we will dispatch the replacement item as soon as the return request is approved. Replacement is subject to product availability. If the product is unavailable, a full refund will be issued.

Order Cancellation and Refund

  • You may cancel an order within 1 hour of placing it, provided it has not been dispatched.
  • Once dispatched, cancellation is not possible. You may refuse the delivery or return the item after receiving it.
  • Cancelled prepaid orders are refunded within 3-5 business days.

Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be issued in the following cases:

  • Items returned with missing components or original packaging.
  • Items that show signs of use or handling beyond inspection.
  • Partial damage where the remaining product is usable.
