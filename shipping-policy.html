Shipping Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Delivery Areas

We currently deliver across Udaipur city and surrounding areas in Rajasthan. During checkout, enter your pincode to check if delivery is available in your area. We are continually expanding our delivery network to serve more locations.

Delivery Timeframes

  • Same-Day Delivery: Orders placed before 2:00 PM within Udaipur city will be delivered the same day between 5:00 PM and 9:00 PM.
  • Next-Day Delivery: Orders placed after 2:00 PM will be delivered the next day in your selected time slot.
  • Scheduled Delivery: You can choose your preferred delivery date and time slot during checkout.
  • Outstation Delivery: For areas outside Udaipur, delivery takes 2-5 business days.

Delivery Charges

  • Free Delivery: Orders above ₹499 qualify for free standard delivery within Udaipur.
  • Standard Delivery: ₹30 for orders below ₹499 within Udaipur.
  • Express Delivery: ₹60 for same-day express delivery (within 3 hours of ordering, subject to availability).
  • Outstation Delivery: Calculated at checkout based on distance and weight.

Delivery Process

  1. After placing your order, you'll receive an order confirmation via email and SMS.
  2. Once your order is packed and dispatched, you'll receive a notification with the estimated delivery time.
  3. Our delivery partner will contact you before delivery to confirm availability.
  4. At the time of delivery, please inspect your items before signing off.
  5. A delivery confirmation notification will be sent once the order is successfully delivered.

Order Tracking

You can track your order in real-time from the My Orders section of your dashboard. We also send tracking updates via SMS and email at key stages: order confirmed, out for delivery, and delivered.

Packaging

We take great care in packaging your products to ensure they arrive in perfect condition. Perishable items are packed in insulated containers with ice packs to maintain freshness during transit. Eco-friendly packaging materials are used wherever possible.

Missed Deliveries

If you are unavailable at the time of delivery, our delivery partner will attempt to reach you by phone. If unreachable, they will try again the next day. After 2 failed attempts, the order may be cancelled and a refund initiated. Please ensure someone is available to receive the delivery or provide alternative instructions.

Special Instructions

You can add special delivery instructions during checkout, such as a preferred time slot, gate code, or landmarks. Our delivery team will do their best to accommodate your requests.
